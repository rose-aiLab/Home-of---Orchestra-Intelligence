## Vision

That everyone — whatever their age, ability, or circumstance — can be supported by intelligence with warmth: gently, and in just the right way.

## Mission

To turn four decades of university AI research into warm, intelligent products for real life — letting people, robots, intelligent systems, and agentic AI play together like the sections of an orchestra, in service of smart healthcare, elder care, children's education, and support for those who need it most.

## Philosophy

Three lines that mirror the name **和 · 智** and our mark:

- **Human at the center.** People remain at the heart of intelligence. *(The small figure in our logo is at once a person and the "i" of Intelligence.)*
- **Harmony, not uniformity (和).** True intelligence isn't everyone sounding the same note — it's many distinct voices, each at its best, resolving into one harmony.
- **Warmth at heart (智).** Technology is not only a tool but a companion with warmth — the heart that sits above the mind.

## About

Orchestra Intelligence (和智) was founded by Professor Runhe Huang. Professor Huang has worked in artificial intelligence since 1982 — across expert systems, knowledge modeling, cognitive computing, intelligent sensing, and AI+IoT systems. Since 2003 he has established and led the AI Research Lab at Hosei University, mentoring a generation of researchers and advancing the use of AI in smart healthcare, elder care, children's education, and support for vulnerable groups.

和智 brings that scholarship home — into everyday life.
